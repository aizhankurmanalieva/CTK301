ABOUT ME

BASIC INFORMATION ABOUT ME

Greetings to all! I am Aizhan, an exchange student from Kyrgyzstan. My major in my home country is legal studies while at ISU I am taking the POL and LGS courses. As for some other facts about me, I speak four languages: English, Russian, Kyrgyz, Turkish. I can play the piano and studied in a musical school for five years. I love travelling and, therefore, I lived in Turkey for several months, visited Europe for a couple of weeks, Russia, neighbouring countries to Kyrgyzstan. In the first half of 2022, I am planning to go to Japan for a couple of weeks. I adore volunteering and believe that a world will be a better place to live in if every person makes at least a small contribution. I volunteered in every country that I visited. Now I am finally in the USA until the end of the semester and I am enjoying every minute of it. I plan to travel within the USA to the extent it will be possible as well. .
